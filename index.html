About Us

Who We Are

Avi Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is a specialized poultry breeding company established to introduce globally recognized poultry genetics into Nepal.

Leading Poultry Genetics in Nepal

We serve as a vital link between international poultry breeding innovation and Nepal's commercial poultry production sector.

Our primary objective is to:

  • Import Grandparent Indian River chicks
  • Maintain them under strict biosecurity standards
  • Produce high-quality Parent Stock chicks
  • Supply breeding stock to hatcheries across Nepal
🎯

Our Mission

To supply genetically superior, healthy, and high-performing Parent Stock chicks that enhance productivity and profitability for Nepalese poultry farmers.

👁️

Our Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted and technically advanced poultry breeding company.

What We Do

Our Core Operations

From import to production, we maintain the highest standards at every step

1

Import of Grandparent Stock

We import Indian River Grandparent day-old chicks from Aviagen, one of the world's leading poultry genetics companies known for innovation, research, and performance.

2

Scientific Rearing & Farm Management

Our Grandparent flocks are raised under:

  • Strict access control
  • Advanced biosecurity systems
  • Structured vaccination programs
  • Professional veterinary supervision
  • Performance monitoring
  • Scientifically balanced nutrition
3

Production of Parent Stock

From our carefully managed Grandparent flocks, we produce high-quality Parent Stock day-old chicks that form the foundation of broiler production in Nepal.

Chairman's Message

"

The poultry industry plays a vital role in Nepal's food security, nutrition, and economic growth. At Avi Nepal Pvt. Ltd., we recognized that sustainable development of this sector begins with one essential foundation — superior genetics.

Our partnership with Aviagen allows us to bring world-class Indian River genetics to Nepal. However, genetics alone is not enough. It must be supported by disciplined management, uncompromising biosecurity, and professional expertise.

Our commitment is to supply Parent Stock chicks that meet international standards while addressing the unique conditions of the Nepalese market. We aim to contribute not only to farmer profitability but also to national poultry self-reliance and long-term industry sustainability.

We believe that by strengthening the foundation of breeding, we strengthen the entire poultry value chain.

Together, we are building the future of Nepal's poultry industry.
